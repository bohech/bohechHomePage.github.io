Short Bio

Xili Wan serves as an Associate Professor and a Master's Supervisor. He graduated from Nanjing University of Technology in 2003 with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology. He obtained a master's degreein Computer Applications from Nanjing University of Technology in 2006. In 2014, he earned a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Missouri, USA.

Research Interests

Research interests include artificial intelligence, cloud computing, big data, and sensor networks, among others.
